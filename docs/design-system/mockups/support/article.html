Set up a review-request campaign

Build an automated cadence that texts and emails your customers to ask for a review — and targets exactly the right contacts.

Updated Jun 28, 2026 5 min read

A review-request campaign automatically reaches out to your customers after a job is done and asks them to leave a review. Once it's active, Oggvo schedules every eligible contact for you — no manual sends.

Before you start

Make sure your funnel is published and at least one review platform (Google, Facebook, …) is connected. You'll also want a few contacts imported and marked Active.

Tip: A contact only receives a campaign once they're active, opted-in, and tag-matched. Eligibility is recomputed whenever you save the campaign or edit a contact — so you never have to re-activate anyone by hand.

Create the campaign

  1. 1Open Campaigns from the left sidebar and click New Campaign → Review Request.
  2. 2Write your message. Insert placeholders like [[first_name]] and [[link]] so each text is personalized.
  3. 3Pick the tags that decide who's targeted. Leave it empty to reach every active contact.
  4. 4Set the send window — Oggvo respects your profile's timezone and business hours.
  5. 5Click Activate. Eligible contacts are scheduled immediately.

Send a test first

Use Send test to preview the SMS on your own phone before contacts get it. Test sends never count against your contacts and use a sandbox number.
