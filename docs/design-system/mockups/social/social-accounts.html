Connect Facebook

You'll be redirected to Facebook to authorize Oggvo to publish on your behalf. We request only posting + page-read permissions.

  1. Sign in and grant access on the provider's consent screen.
  2. Pick the page / account to publish to (Facebook → pages + Instagram; Google → location; LinkedIn → page).
  3. You'll return here with the account connected.

The access token is encrypted at rest (AES-GCM). Disconnect any time.

Disconnect Facebook?

This account will stop being a publish target. Scheduled posts to it will fail. You can reconnect any time.
