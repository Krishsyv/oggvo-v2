Contacts to activate per day

Auto-deactivate after review

Deactivate a contact once they click through to leave a review.

Loads GET /profiles/me, saves POST /profiles/save-settings. Hidden legacy DeactivateRecipientsOnOpen/OnClick always submitted false.

These settings drive the campaign-status banner shown on the Contacts list. When automatic activation is off or paused, that banner warns you that no new contacts are being enrolled.
