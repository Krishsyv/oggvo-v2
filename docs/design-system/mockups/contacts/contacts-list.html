Delete contacts?

This permanently deletes the selected contacts and removes them from all campaigns. This can't be undone.

Activate contacts?

Activate the selected contacts? Activated contacts re-enter the daily review-request cadence; deactivated contacts are removed from it.

Pause campaigns?

All active contacts stop receiving the review-request cadence until you resume. No new contacts are auto-activated while paused.
